Vietnam 300TPH Granite Crushing Plant

Granite sand production line equipment including vibrating feeder, granite jaw crusher, hydraulic cone crusher, granite crusher, circular vibrating screen, high impact crusher (stone shaping machine), has a remarkable stone shaping capacity, is an international, domestic production is widely recognized as the industry's stone grain shape best equipment production lines. Among them, the granite jaw crusher is the key to the entire production line equipment, its biggest feature is the ability to produce high quality grain-shaped stone, with broken and double integer performance; and good grain type of stone buildings can be increased to better mechanical performance, a higher price in the market. Vietnam is an important mining export country in Asia, especially the exportation of Limestone, iron ore, coal, granite and bluestone products. With many years' effort, SBM successfully developed Vietnam market, SBM crushing machineries and milling equipments are very popular in many provinces, like Yen Bai province, Ho Chi Minh City, Nghe An, Tỉnh Quảng Ninh and Hanoi. These mining equipments make up various mining crushing lines, sand making plants and mineral powder production bases.

Customer's Background:

Granite jaw crusher is mainly used to provide high-quality grain-shaped building with a stone crushing stone fields, roads, railways, airports, ports, high-rise buildings. Granite Crusher (Crusher) can handle no more than 120-500 mm in size, not exceeding 320 MPa compressive strength of coarse, medium and fine material ore, rock (granite, limestone, concrete, etc.), widely used hydropower, highway, artificial aggregates, crushing and other industries.
