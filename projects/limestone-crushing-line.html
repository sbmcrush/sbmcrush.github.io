Limestone Crushing Line in Algeria

Limestone crusher is widely used in metallurgy, building materials, electricity, cement, construction, chemical, mining and other industries crushing limestone, slag, fly ash, coal and other materials can be used for dry and wet crushing form in cement production significant early role. Crushed limestone occupies a more important role in crushing the material in cement. Algeria is one of the most important markets in Africa. We have set up an overseas office there and this has benefited lots of local customers. SBM has a good reputation in local market and wins smiles from many customers. Every year we will get large quantity of orders for crushers and grinding mills from Algeria market. SBM's high-quality equipments and customized technical support and after-sale service have earned lots of faith from our customers.

Customer's Background:

Because of backward cement production process and the high demand, had to speed up the production of cement, limestone, coal and other raw materials is one of the major cement manufacturing, and sources of raw materials is very extensive. In 2010, the customer contacts with the sales in SBM headquarter, stating that they need to set up a big limestone crushing line. Their requirements are as follows:

Material: Limestone

Max. Size of the material：800mm

Final products sizes：0-3mm，3-8mm，8-15mm，15-25mm，0-25mm

Capacity：200-250TPH

Limestone jaw crusher is mainly by squeezing between jaw movement to achieve break, generally used in a larger amount of crushing process; high-speed rotary crusher main advantage of rotor speed between the hammer and the liner the impact of the limestone crushing, cone crusher is the movement by squeezing between vertebrae to break material, and Sand is a high-speed rotation of the rotor, the use of materials and linings of high-speed impact to crushing, works and counterattack crusher roughly. In the cement production process, most of the raw material to be crushed, such as limestone, clay, iron ore and coal.
