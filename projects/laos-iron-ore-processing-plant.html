Laos iron ore processing plant

Iron ore crusher is a kind of iron ore were progressively broken chunks of raw iron ore from (500mm or so) to the millimeter level progressively crushing fines machinery and equipment, usually iron ore dry separation machine lean ore enrichment operations, the enrichment of iron ore bonanza into a ball mill grinding, and then choose the way wet separation and extraction of iron ore fines. Laos became one important Southeast Asian market of SBM since 2005, two years later after the Austria SEPON gold ore processing plant started to work. Owe to our good public-praise, more and more Laos customers prefer to cooperate with SBM, and one SBM demo project—one 300TPH iron ore processing plant—begin to work at the beginning of 2014.

Designing processing production: 300TPH

From iron ore production and cost efficiency, the use of cone crusher can effectively reduce production costs. Because cone crusher can provide smaller product size, from the overall process, the adoption of lamination equipment to complete a major iron ore crushing task is the most economical mode of production.

Iron ore crushing process generally uses a feeder, the first break, the second break, screening, crushing, dry separation these processes, for economic reasons, the finer the normally dry sorter into the size of the ore containing iron dry stone was elected proportion is higher. Some small concentrator directly to the fine broken jaw crusher iron ore dry separation of operations, resulting in very serious waste of natural resources. Iron ore production operations head broken jaw crusher is generally used in large-scale production units (especially the foreign large-scale mining) using gyratory crusher. Jaw crusher is the most traditional and most reliable coarse crushing equipment, the most widely used range of applications. 2013 jaw crusher manufacturing processes and technology is very mature, so long as the use of regular factory product, are able to meet the design and production requirements.
