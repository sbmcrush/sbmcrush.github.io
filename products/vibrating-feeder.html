Vibrating Feeder

Vibrating feeder in the production process, can the massive, granular materials from silos in uniform, regular, continuous feeding device to go into the subject in the sand and gravel production line can be broken machinery continuous uniform feed classification. Vibrating Feeder is a kind of linear-direction feeding equipment, in the mineral ore &rocks processing plants, it can send materials to crusher evenly and continuously. At the same time, it can screen materials roughly, increasing the practical handling capacity of the whole production line.

Vibrating Feeder Feature

  • Stable performance &Reliable operation.
  • Easy to adjust &Easy to maintain
  • Long span life &No pollution with closed body
  • Good performance-conditioning, it can be used in any severe environments.

Vibrating feeder during operation should always check the amplitude of the vibration of the motor current and the motor surface temperature uniformity around the requirements of the amplitude of the vibration motor current stability, such as unusual circumstances, should immediately stop processing. Vibration motor bearing lubrication is the key to the entire feeder working properly, in the course of the bearing lubrication regularly, once every two months, filling, filling the hot season once a month, once every six months to overhaul motor, replace the bearing.

Feeder (vibrating feeders) Applications: massive, granular materials from silos in uniform, continuous feeding to the affected device. In the sand and gravel production line for continuous crusher crusher evenly feeding avoid clogging by the discharge port. Designed for pre-crushing and screening coarse crusher evenly conveying bulk materials design. Feeder with double eccentric shaft vibration exciter structural features to ensure that equipment can withstand the impact of the whereabouts of the bulk material, to a large feed capacity. Can be massive, granular materials in the production process even from silos, the timing, continuously to go to the affected device, thus preventing the feeding device because of feed produced uneven crash phenomenon, extended equipment life. Feeders can be divided into steel structure and grate structure, steel structure of feeder used for sand and gravel production line, all the material evenly into the broken equipment; grate structure feeder can be rough screening of materials, in the preparation of the system is more economical in the crushing and screening has been used as essential equipment.
