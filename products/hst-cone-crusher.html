Cone Crusher Feature

Cone crusher is an advanced, high-power, high crushing ratio, high productivity, hydraulic crusher. The machine combines the world's advanced level of high energy crusher, it is crushing speed, eccentricity (stroke) and the successful combination of high performance crushing cavity design. The machine is in the digestion and absorption of the countries with a basis of 80 years of international advanced level of various types of cone crusher on developed into not only improves the production capacity and crushing power, but also expand the range of applications, from limestone to basalt, from stone production to a variety of ore crushing, crushing can provide unparalleled performance in a variety of pieces, crushing, ultra-crushing job.

  • High production capacity and excellent grain type of output product.
  • Special bearing design for much more carrying capacity and longer life.
  • Enhanced body design makes it quite reliability.
  • Easy to adjust discharging opening and to maintain by using optimized hydraulic lubrication station.

Cone crusher is widely used in mining industry, metallurgical industry, construction industry, road building industry, chemical industry and silicate industry, suitable for crushing hard and medium-hard ores and rocks, such as iron ore, limestone, copper ore, quartz, granite, sandstone, etc.
