High pressure grinding mill roller device usage time over 500 hours or so to replace the roller, the roller of each rolling bearing kit must be cleaned, damaged parts should be replaced, fuel pumps and hand tools available grease gun. MTM trapezium grinder mill use in the process, there should be a fixed charge of personnel, the operator must have a certain level of technology. Before installing high pressure mill operator must carry out the necessary technical training to understand the principle of the performance of the high pressure mill, familiar rules. After the MTM trapezium grinder mill used for a period of time, maintenance should be carried while roller grinding ring blade overhaul replacement of wearing parts such as processing, grinding roller device before and after the use of the connection bolts and nuts should be carefully examined to see if there are loose phenomenon, whether to add a little grease.
