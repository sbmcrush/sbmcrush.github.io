T130X Reinforced Ultrafine Mill

Grinding mill equipment more representative, more also is using ultrafine mill, it requires the feeder, conveyor, bucket elevator, etc. to form a complete production line in the work process, the implementation of pipeline operations. The degree of automation of high-pressure micro-grinding process is relatively high, after mineral granules into the mill, the mill is finished fine, coarse grinding process is returned to continue grinding. In order to improve the quality of mineral utilization and finished generally milling equipment are equipped with coarse sorting reflux regrinding process.

T130X Reinforced Ultrafine Mill Feature

  • Adopt flexible connection between the mainframe and the classifier, avoiding too much vibrating.
  • Using Germany Flender reducer ensure that it has reliable performance.
  • Classifier applies high-density blade and it is easy to adjust the by equipping frequency control system.
  • Applying dust bypass collector, it won't bring environment pollution.
  • Optimized design make powder centralized collection and packing become easy.

Grinding mill equipment includes: raymond mill, powder mill, high pressure micro-grinding, high-strength mill, hanging roller mill, ball mill, ceramic ball, tilting milling machines, vertical milling machines . In addition, the impact crusher is also listed as a coarse grinding equipment, which is mainly used in sand production line. Although there are many milling equipment, but its essence is the same mill, the fine-grained minerals are ground into powder materials.

T130X reinforced ultrafine mill is a new facility designed by SBM expert basing on statistical analysis and a large number of suggestions from users and the innovative design of the super pressure trapezoidal mill. It has superseded and optimized in the structural characteristics of trapezoidal MTM mill, and it also forms its own unique characteristics.
