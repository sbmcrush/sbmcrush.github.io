PY Cone Crusher

PY series cone crusher can be used as the first choice for granite crushing. This series of cone crusher designed for crushing medium hardness of various minerals and rocks and design, easy to adjust, reliable structure, high production efficiency, the use of the economy. In particular equipment installed spring safety system as the overload protection device that can ensure a foreign body iron through the crushing chamber along by it, thus avoiding damage to the device. Device uses dry oil or water in two-tight manner, so that the powder can be isolated and lubrication to ensure reliable operation of the machine.

Cone Crusher Feature

PY series cone crusher crushing granularity demands more attention, taking into account to minimize the wear and tear of the device. Device uses laminated crushing principle, making crushing and screening more uniform product size, flakiness stones reduced, thereby significantly improving the product in the proportion of the cube. Direct contact with the equipment in order to reduce losses, cone crusher designers follow the "more crushing and less grinding" principle, through the cavity design and optimization of a reasonable combination of speed and stroke, the full use of the material laminated crushing principle, to minimize stone and machine , preferably extending the life of the equipment.

  • High productivity; high quality.
  • Less machine stop time.
  • Easy maintenance and low cost.
  • Unique crushing ability in primary, secondary and tertiary crushing.

Adopting technology from the world, PY Series spring cone crusher has an excellent performance in secondary crushing and tertiary crushing process and it has become the ideal cone crusher for small capacity crushing plants of mineral ore processing industry and rocks processing industry.
