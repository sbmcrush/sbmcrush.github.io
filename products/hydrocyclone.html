Hydrocyclone

Hydrocyclone

Hydrocyclone wear is one of the most important use of indicators. Under the sediment cone wear mouth and its maximum phase, in order to prevent wear and tear, we recommend that the cyclone body lining and device wear-resistant materials interchangeable parts, ready to be replaced. Wear-resistant materials commonly used in rubber, polyurethane, silicon carbide and the like. Hydrocyclone has been extensively used for closed circuit grinding and classification system , thickening, desliming, dewatering , tailings filling, damming, recovery processes in ferrous, nonferrous metal and nonmetal mine industries, and is deeply popular with customers due to high classification efficiency, simple structure, large throughput, and small occupied area.

Hydrocyclone Feature

Many parameters of hydrocyclone with structure, process and operational aspects of the nature of the feed, and often interrelated, mutual restraint, easy to adjust and control, which is the reason it is important in a wide range of difficult applications. Now its main parameters related summarized below.

  • Simple structure and there is no moving parts.
  • High capacity and high classifying efficient.
  • Easy to disassembly and easy to maintain.
  • Low investment cost and taking little space.

Hydrocyclone is a use of centrifugal force of a structure type pulp grading equipment, but also the hydraulic classification of the device. China's first manufactured by Yunnan Tin Corporation and hydrocyclones, to the early 1960s began to try to iron ore processing plant, but because it wear quickly, life is short, easy to control the process parameters, and therefore not able to promote the long-term . Over the past years, with the improvement and the use of hydro-cyclone structure and manufacturing materials automatic control device to further highlight the advantages, only gradually popularized. As early as 30 years ago, has been widely used in foreign ore dressing plant hydrocyclone, basically replacing the spiral classifier, while China is still using spiral classifier based, which can not but arouse our attention.
