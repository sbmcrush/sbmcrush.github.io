SCM Ultrafine Mill Feature

  • High Output and low energy consumption.
  • High fineness and flexible adjustment. The grinding pressure is high, the hardness of the material can be adapted to a variety of grinding work.
  • Safe and Reliable. SCM ultrafine grinding mill dust removal effect is entirely up to the national emission standards.
  • Clean and environmental friendly. Effectively reducing the noise at work, and avoid dust contamination and air pollution.

SCM ultrafine grinding mill can crush talc, marble, limestone, silica, clay and other non-metallic and metallic materials, often referred to as high-pressure high-pressure micro-grinding mill, high pressure grinding short, is an ultra-fine grinder. Pulverized material required by the elevator into the storage bin, and then there are elevator into the grinding chamber, and then by the shovel blade into rubbing crushed between the roller and grinding ring, fans will be blown into the grinding chamber, blowing the powder into the analysis and internal, were winnowing, size does not meet the requirements of another regrinding, qualified to enter the finished product cyclone powder collector, and air separation from the discharge port is finished.

SCM Ultrafine Mill Application

SCM Super-micro mill is mainly used to produce fine powder and super fine powder. It is applicable to non-flammable and non-explosive materials with hardness less than 6, such as calcite, chalk, limestone, dolomite, kaolin, bentonite, talcum, talc, magnesite, illite, pyrophyllite, vermiculite, sepiolite, etc.
