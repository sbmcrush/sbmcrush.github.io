Flotation machine is the most effective equipment and optional silver with gold equipment, gold ore and silver ore in the ground with water and essential medicines stirred tank tune into pulp, the injection began stirring slurry tank to the pulp introducing air, to form a large number of bubbles, some not easily wetted by water, i.e., generally referred to as hydrophobic mineral particles attached to air bubbles, and the bubbles float together with the pulp surface, a bubble is formed mineralized layer, while others readily water wet, i.e. generally called hydrophilic mineral particles are not attached to the bubbles remaining in the pulp, containing a specific mineral mineralization bubble discharge, so as to achieve the purpose of dressing.
