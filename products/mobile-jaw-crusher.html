Mobile Jaw Crusher

Mobile jaw crusher (jaw mobile crusher) is a newly developed crusher, wide range of uses, reasonable design, there is a strong function of mobile crusher, mobile crusher can be a device in the rugged mountains, for customers to reduce costs, safe operation, completely standing in the customer's point of view to the design, but also used in metallurgy, chemical industry, building materials, and other industries, is currently one of the best mobile crusher station equipment.

Mobile Jaw Crusher Feature

  • Hydraulic-drive feeding plate is convenient and reliable.
  • Easy to control by equipping with high-tech LCD touch screen and PLC control system.
  • Feeder grates pre-screen scrap and excurrent material can be conveyed by side belt or main belt either. Mobile Crusher characteristics are very much during the job greatly reduces the labor time and labor intensity, reducing material consumption hours, reasonable design, easy maintenance.
  • Belt conveyors and Magnetic iron separator are easy to use. Jaw mobile crusher operation time is short, high efficiency, different crushing equipment were installed on a separate removable chassis, short wheelbase, small turning radius, flexible travel and work in the general area of the highway.

Jaw mobile crusher station principle is very simple, the main and then transferred by conveyor into the crusher, and then after a preliminary crushing jaw crusher, and then form a closed system through circular vibrating screen, so that a process to achieve the final steps to achieve continuous operation.

Portable mobile jaw crusher is developed according to novel series stone crushing equipment's design idea, the adaption of ship form structure lowers the chassis; reduce the weight and volume of the plant; and makes it easy for turning and transportation; the fashionable design and standardized mass production fully meet customers' requirements.
