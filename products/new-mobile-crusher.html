Working Principle of Mobile Crusher

Mobile crusher is a new rock crushing equipment, greatly expanded the concept of crushing operations in the field. Mobile crusher is designed to the customer's position, to eliminate the crushing site, environmental barriers to customers as the primary crushing operations solutions, and provide customers with a cost-effective operation of the project hardware. Excellent performance of mobile crusher station: PE series high performance crusher, crusher integrated feeder and vibrating screen, belt conveyor under the screen, vibrating screen, crusher integrated vehicle installation, steering axle traction, easy highway transport and venues in depth, on-board installation support, equipment stationed fast and convenient venue, vehicle-mounted electric motor and control box integration.

Mobile Crusher Feature

Mobile crusher station equipment with a reasonable matching, unobstructed discharge the entire line, reliable, easy to operate, energy efficient features. Especially good mobility, can extend with the raw material or the construction site, and can be a variety of combinations to meet the needs of different materials.

  • Simple structure, easy maintenance.
  • Stable performance, high capacity.
  • Even final particles and high crushing ratio.
  • Adopt advanced manufacturing technique and high-end materials.

Portable mobile jaw crusher is developed according to novel series stone crushing equipment's design idea, the adaption of ship form structure lowers the chassis; reduce the weight and volume of the plant; and makes it easy for turning and transportation; the fashionable design and standardized mass production fully meet customers' requirements.

Application of Mobile Crusher

Mobile crushing plant (mobile crusher) is mainly used in metallurgy, chemical industry, building materials, utilities and other materials often need to move processing operations, especially for highways, railways, hydropower engineering and other stone work flow, real customers to create a more new business opportunities and reduce production costs.
