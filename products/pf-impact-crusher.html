PF Impact Crusher Feature

By absorbing the advanced technology from the world, we researched and designed PF series impact crusher. It can be used to deal with materials whose size below 500mm and whose compression strength less than 360Mpa. Thus, it is widely used in many industries, like chemical, metallurgy, road and bridge construction and sand making and so on.

  • High efficient Long service life.
  • Strong structure Steady performance.
  • Good final products Discharging opening is adjustable.
  • No-board connection Easy to disassembly and maintain.

Impact crusher can process the side length of 100 to 500 mm below the material, its compressive strength up to 350 MPa, with a crushing ratio, after crushing the material was cubic particles, etc., suitable for crushing hard materials, such as cement limestone broken, with the production capacity, the material advantages of small size.
