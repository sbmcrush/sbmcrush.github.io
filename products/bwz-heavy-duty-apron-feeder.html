BWZ Heavy Duty Apron Feeder

Heavy apron feeder is a short distance to reprint transportation equipment, systems commonly used in the early break to reprint materials warehouse pressure conditions of work, mainly by the drive spindle apparatus, chain belt device, supporting device, tensioning device, composition frame, tailgate hopper section. BWZ series heavy duty apron feeder designed by SBM is one new type high-efficiency conveying equipments. It absorbs SBM decades years' experience in designing &manufacturing conveying machines and the advanced technology of the world. This apron feeder is especially suitable for short-distance transmission.

BWZ Heavy Duty Apron Feeder Feature

  • Use alloying-steel plate, the machine has long service life and requires no maintenance.
  • It has high tension and it is have very stable performance by using track.
  • For its simple structure, it is easy to maintain.
  • BWZ series heavy duty apron feeder has wide application and the temperature, moisture and the environment have little or no effect on it.

BWZ heavy duty apron feeder works: power motor and reducer coupling through the drive chain wheel rotation, meshing through sprocket and chain, drag chain plates do continuous linear motion. Chain plate by the support and care sprocket wheel mounted on the support frame, and engage correct it by adjusting the tensioning device chain and sprocket, aim to complete delivery of materials.

Heavy plate feeder is widely used in mining, metallurgy, building materials and coal and other industrial sectors. It is mainly used for silos and warehouses funnel below a certain pressure, the uniform continuous conveyor crushing equipment and screening equipment to a variety of large bulk density of the material a short distance, especially in the early pieces by following more appropriate. It is not only suitable for handling coarse materials, for fine-grained material also adapt. In harsh environments, heavy duty, material changes in the size composition of the material, temperature, viscosity, frost, snow or ice junction effects have greater adaptability. But even quasi-reliable feeding amount, the machine can be installed horizontally, can also be tilted installation, transportation up to a maximum angle of 23 °.
